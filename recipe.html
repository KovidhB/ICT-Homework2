Recipe

  1. cut the chicken breast into strips
  2. mix the spices and sauces together
  3. cover the chopped chicken breast with the mixture
  4. leave the tenders in the fridge for 2-5 hours
  5. crack and mix 2-3 eggs in a bowl
  6. pour some corn flour on a plate
  7. after 2-5 hours take the tenders out
  8. pour some cooking oil in a pan and on the stove
  9. keep the pan on the stove carefully
  10. dip the tenders in corn flour then in egg mixture then again in the corn flour and then in the pan
  11. repeat step 10 till all the chicken tenders are in the pan
  12. then carefully take the cooked tenders out
  13. put the cooked chicken tenders on a tissue
  14. pour the dip in a bowl
  15. enjoy!
